Our Story

About Our Church

Rooted in Lagos, Reaching the Nations

Fullness of Christ Church was founded in 2002 by Bishop Emmanuel & Dr. Faith Okonkwo with a vision to establish a place of total transformation for every man and woman who walks through the doors. Starting with a congregation of 12 families in a rented hall in Surulere, the church has grown into a vibrant family of over 4,500 members spread across Lagos and beyond.

Our church stands on the pillars of the Word, Worship, Welfare, and Witness. Every department is dedicated to equipping, empowering, and sending believers to make a difference in their families, workplaces, and communities across Nigeria.

We are proudly located at 14 Redemption Crescent, Lekki Phase 1, Lagos State ‚Äî a landmark of grace in one of Lagos's most vibrant communities. We also have branches in Ajah, Victoria Island, and Ikeja.

Our Vision
To see every person living in the fullness of Christ's purpose ‚Äî spiritually empowered, emotionally whole, and Kingdom-advancing.
Our Mission
To preach the undiluted Gospel, make disciples, heal the broken-hearted, and raise generation-defining leaders for God's Kingdom in Nigeria.
Our Values
Faith. Integrity. Excellence. Love. Prayer. Community. We pursue God's presence above all and serve with joyful hearts.
